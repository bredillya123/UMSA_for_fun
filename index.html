Rector's Welcome Message

Dear friends!

Welcome to the website of one of the oldest institutions of higher education - the Ukrainian Medical Stomatological Academy!

During the long path of development and formation, the Academy has prepared and released more than one generation of doctors of various specialities into the vast world of medicine. They have made significant contributions to the development of both science and practical healthcare. More than one generation of the academy graduates had studied at the Academy during its hundred-year-old history. Each of them increased the fame of their Alma Mater with scientific and practical achievements.

The words of the Medical Oath were first heard in the walls of the Kharkiv Medical Institute in 1921. And since then, the first specialized Higher Education Institution for the training of dental professionals was destined for a long life, full of hard work. Currently, the academy has more than 5,000 students, including 1,350 foreign citizens from 45 different countries. Our graduates work all over the world and this is the outpost of the academy.

We are proud that for many years we have managed to keep in our team a lot of outstanding specialists well-known far beyond Ukraine - teachers, scientists of the "old scientific school", as well as those who continue to follow the age-old traditions of higher education - to make scientific discoveries in medicine and to involve students in their research, as well as to prepare future leading lights of medicine for work at the academy. So, we have people to learn from and what to learn, and most importantly, we should all to work together for the future!

I wish future entrants to take a balanced approach to choosing the profession of a doctor, and students - to succeed in their studies and fruitful work in the future for the good of Ukraine!

The UMSA website will help you to continuously search for necessary information. Good luck!
